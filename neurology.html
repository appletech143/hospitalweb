Neurology Department

Expert care for neurological conditions and disorders

Our Expertise

At SAI HOSPITAL's Neurology Department, we provide comprehensive care for all neurological conditions. Our team of experienced neurologists uses state-of-the-art technology for diagnosis and treatment.

Services We Offer

  • Neurological Consultations
  • EEG (Electroencephalogram)
  • EMG (Electromyography)
  • Stroke Treatment and Management
  • Epilepsy Management
  • Movement Disorders Treatment
  • Headache and Migraine Treatment
  • Multiple Sclerosis Care

Our Facilities

Our department is equipped with advanced diagnostic and therapeutic equipment including:

  • Advanced Neuroimaging
  • EEG Laboratory
  • EMG Testing Facility
  • Dedicated Stroke Unit
  • Neurorehabilitation Center

Meet Our Team

Our team consists of highly qualified neurologists, neurosurgeons, and specialized nursing staff dedicated to providing the best possible care for our patients.
