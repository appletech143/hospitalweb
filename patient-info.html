Frequently Asked Questions

What items should I bring for admission?
Bring comfortable clothing, toiletries, any current medications, insurance card, and identification documents. Avoid bringing valuables to the hospital.
How can I get my medical records?
Submit a request to our medical records department with valid ID. Processing typically takes 3-5 business days. Records can be collected in person or sent securely via email.
What are the parking facilities?
We provide free parking for patients and visitors in our designated parking area. Valet parking is available at the main entrance for a nominal fee.
